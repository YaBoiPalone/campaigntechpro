Indian Election Voter Analytics 2024

2024 Election Scale: 2024 General Elections saw 65.79% overall voter turnout across 543 constituencies with 970 million eligible voters.
State-wise Variations: West Bengal recorded highest turnout at 79.29%, while Bihar had 56.19% - showing critical state-wise variations.
Digital Panna Pramukh: Micro-targeting by demographics helped BJP replicate 'panna pramukh' strategy digitally across 543 distinct constituencies.
Historic Gender Parity: Female voter turnout (65.78%) nearly matched male turnout (65.80%) - first time in Indian election history.
GIS Campaign Intelligence: GIS-mapped voter analytics revealed ward-level support patterns, enabling precise ground campaign planning.
Sources: ECI 2024 data, Google Transparency Report, Meta Ad Library
